Waffle Weave Drying Towel MINI

At last, the easy to handle, 'MINI' dehydrator.  At 60cm x 40cm, this drying towel will remove water from a vehicle quickly and efficiently whilst being easier to handle than it's 60cm x 90cm big brother.

 Unlike a Chamois, a Microfibre Drying Towel is properly machine washable, making it a much safer alternative.

Unlike a Leather or PVC chamois, no debris is trapped in the fibres and microfibre will not harbour bacteria like those materials, especially because it can be washed with an anti-bacterial wash & dried in the same way most fabrics are.

Unlike a chamois, in a Wash, any trapped debris is easily removed so no reintroduction of that debris occurs the next time you use the towel to dry your painted surfaces.

The Waffle Weave Drying Towel is a very high quality, 300 gsm, 60 x 40cm NanoFibre™ cloth recommended for use on all exterior surfaces such as paint, glass and plastics.

The NanoFibre™ drying towel features hand sewn pure NanoFibre™ edging, not rayon or cotton and no edge-less heat stamping.  Consisting of 80% nano polyester fibre for ultra efficient cleaning and absorption around a (20%) polyamide core, our Waffle Weave Drying Towel has superior quck water absorption characteristics.

Recommended Uses

- Completely dry or slightly damp to very quickly remove water from any exterior surface

- Dry to remove lubricant from freshly clayed surfaces.

NanoFibre Care

Care of all microfibre and nanofibre products involves removal of dirt and debris as soon as possible with gentle cleaners.  Final Inspection recommend 'Peppermint' anti-bacterial nanofibre wash solution, using no more than 60deg water temperature.
